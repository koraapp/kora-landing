Start with KORA

AEM websites can be complex and time consuming. With KORA, start near the finish line with out-of-box components. Iterate, change styles, and grow your component library for an unlimited number of websites. Stop duplicating components and bring your team together with a more efficient, lasting strategy for AEM.

KORA Component Library

From simple typography components to complex navigation and media components, KORA is the perfect starting line for any website. Need a quick microsite? KORA is all you need.

KORA Design System

Seamlessly integrated with the component library, designers can quickly create new styles using Sketch files and powerful Sass documents to share with the rest of the organization.

KORA Launch

This application allows non-developers to quickly create local instances of AEM, modify UI style, add content and prototype all within AEM itself. Run a package export and hand off to developers when the time is right.

Save time, cut cost and reduce time-to-market

KORA allows teams to move quickly. Spend less time developing and authoring, and more time utilizing the full potential of Adobe Experience Cloud. With KORA, focus on your product MVP and launch quickly so you can better understand your users' needs.

Based on Atomic Design

The strategy behind KORA is to breakdown interfaces seen on every website and deliver a baseline set of components that are useful to everyone who uses AEM. This means that KORA can represent 100% of the component makeup for a basic website or landing page, requiring little to nothing from your developers. For e-commerce or application-driven websites, starting with KORA places your team well ahead of the curve.
