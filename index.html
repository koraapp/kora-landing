Start with KORA

AEM websites can be complex and time consuming. With KORA, start near the finish line with out-of-box components. Iterate, change styles, and grow your component library for an unlimited number of websites. Stop duplicating components and bring your team together with a more efficient, lasting strategy for AEM.

Your single source of truth for AEM

Based on Atomic Design principles, KORA’s Design System includes the essentials for any website. Seamlessly integrated with the component library, designers can quickly create new styles using Sketch files and powerful Sass documents to share with the rest of the organization.

Essential OOB components for any AEM website

The bread and butter of KORA, the component library is how all AEM websites begin. From simple typography components to complex navigation and media components, KORA is the perfect starting line for any website. Need a quick microsite? KORA is all you need.

Your team, empowered

Start designing and authoring before development outside KORA is needed. This IDE application allows non-developers to quickly spin up local instances of AEM, modify UI style, add content and prototype all within AEM itself. Run a package export and hand off to developers when the time is right.

For Teams, Not Individuals

Created by designers, authors and developers who use AEM everyday, KORA bridges the gap between AEM itself and the people that use it.

UX Designers

Design for the product not the CMS - With KORA’s shared design system, designers understand and control every detail of the component library. Rapid wireframe and prototype based on existing components. Grow and expand the library by adding new components that meet the needs of your business.

Developers

Spend time developing what matters - At some point, all AEM developers have wished for a group of basic authoring components they didn’t have to build themselves. With KORA, work only on components that drive the business by expanding the library to include the needs for your business.

Authors

A unified, dedicated UX - Dialog boxes are no longer an afterthought. With consistent UI patterns, naming conventions and easy to use functionality, KORA puts the author first. In a content heavy world, authors are needed now more than ever.

Product Owners

Know your product - The job of KORA is to provide the essential tools of any AEM website. Easily understand where KORA ends and new development begins. Respond quickly to business needs by iterating, launching or testing new websites.

Save time, cut cost and reduce time-to-market.

We estimate that KORA’s components make up 65% of a typical data driven website. For a basic microsite or landing page, KORA may be all you need. In addition, KORA is designed to be used on every site. Ultimately, KORA allows teams to move quickly.
